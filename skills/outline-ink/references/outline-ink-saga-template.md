> Scope: multi-book / multi-season structure — the throughline across the entire series
> Build section by section. Do not generate all at once.

---

## Agent instruction

Start with the central promise: what does the whole series add up to? Everything else must serve that.
If the author doesn't know yet → "to define" and continue.
When the arc for each book is sketched, verify internal consistency before moving on.
